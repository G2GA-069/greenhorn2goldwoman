Usage Guide

How to Use This Reference

For exam revision: Print it. Highlight liberally. The sections mirror the chapter order, so cross-reference back to the story whenever a concept feels abstract.

For interview prep: The formula sheet and glossary at the back are your best friends. Know every formula cold. Be able to explain any glossary term in one sentence.

For on-the-job reference: Keep the PDF on your desktop. When you encounter a concept in a model or pitchbook, search the document. Everything is there.

For the curious: Read it cover to cover once. Then never again — unless you need to look something up. That is the point of a reference document.
